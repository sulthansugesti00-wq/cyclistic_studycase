# Cyclistic bike analysis 
A data analysis Project examining the behaviour between casual and annual membership customer to develop a targeted conversion strategy.

## Tools 
- R (dplyr, lubridate, ggplot2, readr)

## Key findings
- Around 32% of 2025 transactions are commuter based on model with assumption
- Electric bike consist of 60% of the whole fleet therefore cannot identify bike preference due to bias
- 3 Highest average ride with casual proportion are Friday, Saturday, and Sunday with Saturday having the most casual proportion.
- 3 highest commuter station are, Clinton St & Washington Blvd, Kingsbury St & Kinzie St, Canal St& Madison St
- Peak hour for commuters are 4 PM - 6 PM, with 5PM having the highest casual proportion than the rest
